Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a favorite in every home let us show you why!

What will I need?

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • worstecher sauce
  • Lawry's seasoning
  • Garlic salt
  • paprika
  • ragu spaghetti sauce
  • a pan to cook the ground beef
  • a pot for the noodles

I got it all! But now what?

  1. Put your pot on the stove with water to boil
  2. Cook your ground beef on high with all the listed spices
  3. Once the meat is browned, drain the meat to remove grease
  4. Once boiling, add noodles and cook for 11 minutes
  5. keep meat on low heat and add ragu spaghetti sauce
  6. Once the noodles are done drain and return to pot
  7. Mix noodles and sauce together
  8. Enjoy!
