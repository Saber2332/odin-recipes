Macaroni and Cheese

    Macaroni and cheese is a favorite in almost every home, with the holidays just around the corner lets get ready to create something the entire family will enjoy

    What will you need?

    • Evaporated Milk
    • Velveeta Cheese
    • Cheddar Cheese
    • elbow noodles
    • Salt
    • pepper
    • A pot big enough to cook the noodles

    Lets get started!

    1. Set your water to boil
    2. Once boiling, add your noodles
    3. Cook for 8 minutes stirring occasionally
    4. Once finished drain your noodles
    5. After draining, return noodles to the original pot and return to stove on medium to low heat
    6. Pour in some evaporated milk, this will prevent your mac from being dry
    7. Drop in some velveeta and cheddar cheese, stirring it in as it melts
    8. Once you're happy with the amount of cheese add salt + pepper and mix well
    9. Enjoy!
